A Hackerspace for the
Southern Illinois Community

We have the ambitious goal of establishing the first ever hackerspace in Southern Illinois. Our vision is to provide an awesome venue giving members access to cutting edge technology, and offering crash courses on tech to empower our community to do great things.

Build

Make the next big thing

SIHackers is working on aquiring hardware for our members to hack on. Maybe you came here with a big dream, maybe you took one of our classes and want to learn more, or maybe you just like tinkering. Whatever your motivations, our goal is to offer you a range of technology from commoditiy hardware to cutting edge tech and provide a social environment for you to hack in. Here is a short version of our shopping list:

  • Individual Desktops
  • Preconfigured servers running popular databases and webservers
  • Clusters for Distributed Computing
  • Mobile Devices
    • Android
    • Windows
    • IOS
    • Phones
    • Tablets
  • RaspberryPis
  • Arduinos
  • Microsoft Kinects
  • And MUCH MUCH more

Learn

Take crash courses

The industry is constantly changing. New technologies come and go quickly, with each iteration bringing new and exciting features. SIHackers aims to offer crash courses over new technology, bringing you up to speed and getting you ready to hack away on it. We want you to create cool things, and our courses empower you to do just that. A quick rundown of some exciting courses we hope to offer:

  • Mobile Development
    • Android
    • Windows
    • IOS
  • GitHub
  • Raspberry Pi
  • Web Development
    • HTML5/JavaScript/CSS3
    • HTML5/PureCss/YUI
    • Canvas/Websockets
    • Server Technology
  • Distributed Computing and Big Data
  • And MUCH MUCH more

Compete

Competitive Programming

We aim to offer a social environment for competing in competive programming matches with people from Southern Illinois and around the world. We aim to host local programming competitions covering materials from our crash courses as well as offer a venue for competing in online competitions. Paired with our crash courses, we hope to offer a variety of different competition styles to suit everyone's abilities.

  • Algorithms
  • User Design
  • Debugging
  • In House Competitions
    • Web Development
    • Mobile Development
    • Game Development
  • Proof of Concept
  • And MUCH MUCH more
